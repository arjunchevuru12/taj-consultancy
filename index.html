About TAJ Consultancy

A practical partner for organized, real-time employment operations

TAJ Consultancy, short for Talent Acquisition Journey, was created to make employment operations more structured, clear, and effective. We support businesses that need better control over recruitment and workforce coordination, and we help consultants and employees stay aligned through a user-friendly system.

Our approach is simple: define roles clearly, keep information moving in real time, and make every module easy to use. This creates smoother operations, clearer accountability, and better visibility for everyone involved.
